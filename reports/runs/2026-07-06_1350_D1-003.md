## Result

`docs/CONFIDENCE_AND_RANKING.md` is now a frozen v1.0.0 contract. Evidence coverage, candidate fit, and calibrated confidence are three distinct, never-merged outputs with fixed ranges and meanings. Six abstention gates (`coverage_floor`, `verifier_disagreement`, `out_of_distribution`, `fit_floor`, `margin_floor`, `contradiction_block`) have frozen ids, strict boundary semantics, an outcome mapping onto `insufficient_evidence` / `no_good_reference_match`, and a deterministic decision precedence. Threshold values are explicitly provisional configuration until D6-002 calibration. Uncalibrated confidence must be labeled "provisional — uncalibrated" and never rendered as a probability; the only uncalibrated display is a qualitative heuristic band.
