# Evaluation

Evaluation is a first-class product artifact, not a final polish step.

Cases must cover:

- canonical excerpts from known sources;
- adjacent cosmologies that share vocabulary;
- hybrid and syncretic texts;
- quotation without endorsement;
- criticism and negation;
- multiple or fictional voices;
- sparse non-cosmological prose;
- novel and out-of-distribution cosmologies;
- value-bearing passages with and without defensible MAI matches.

Human-reviewed labels store reviewer identity, rationale, evidence spans, acceptable candidate set, and whether abstention is required. Keep the calibration split held out from prompt and threshold tuning.
